Brand Story

Our heritage, mission, and values

Our Heritage

Founded in 2026, Amjad & Hazli represents a new generation of chartered accountants who blend traditional expertise with modern innovation. Our firm was established on the principle that financial guidance should be both accessible and sophisticated.

Mission

To provide exceptional financial services that empower businesses and individuals to achieve their financial goals with confidence and clarity. We believe in building lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and excellence.

Vision

To be the most trusted financial advisory partner in Malaysia, known for our unwavering commitment to client success and our innovative approach to traditional accounting services.

Core Values

Integrity — We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our dealings.

Excellence — We strive for perfection in every engagement.

Innovation — We embrace modern solutions while respecting tradition.

Partnership — We view our clients as partners in their financial journey.

Brand Positioning

Amjad & Hazli occupies a unique position in the Malaysian accounting landscape—bridging the gap between boutique personal service and comprehensive professional expertise. We are the choice for discerning clients who value both competence and character.

03

Clear Space & Sizing

Maintaining logo integrity

Clear Space

Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo to ensure visibility and impact. The minimum clear space is equal to the height of the "A" in the monogram on all sides.

Minimum Sizes

To maintain legibility and visual integrity, observe these minimum size requirements:

Application Minimum Width
Digital (screen) 48px
Print (small) 15mm
Print (standard) 25mm

Favicon Usage

For very small applications (favicons, app icons), use the simplified monogram variant. This ensures the logo remains recognizable at small sizes.

Do's & Don'ts

Maintaining brand consistency

Do

  • Use the gold monogram on navy backgrounds
  • Maintain minimum clear space around logo
  • Use white logo variants on dark backgrounds
  • Keep minimum size of 48px for digital
  • Download assets from official sources
  • Use approved color combinations
  • Maintain aspect ratio when scaling

Don't

  • Rotate or skew the logo
  • Change the gold or navy colors
  • Add effects like shadows or outlines
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Recreate or modify the monogram
  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Use unapproved color combinations

Incorrect Usage Examples

The following are examples of how NOT to use the brand identity:

No rotation

No stretching

No color changes

No busy backgrounds
