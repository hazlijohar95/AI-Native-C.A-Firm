First Principles

What is tax compliance, really?

Tax compliance means meeting all your tax obligations—filing returns on time, paying the right amount, and claiming eligible deductions. It's about staying legal while minimizing your tax burden.

1

Tax Planning

We review your business structure and transactions throughout the year to identify tax-saving opportunities. Proactive planning ensures you claim all eligible deductions and incentives.

2

Compliance Monitoring

We track all tax deadlines—SST filings, corporate tax returns, withholding tax. You'll never miss a deadline or face penalties. We ensure you're always compliant.

3

Filing Preparation

We prepare all tax returns and filings with accuracy. Every number is verified, every deduction is maximized, every form is complete. Nothing is left to chance.

4

Submission & Follow-up

Our Approach

How we solve real problems

Real scenarios from businesses like yours

SST Registration

New Business Setup

The Problem: A new e-commerce business wasn't sure if they needed SST registration. They were selling online and didn't know if their services were taxable. They risked penalties for non-compliance.

Our Solution

Assessed their business model and determined SST registration was required. Handled the entire registration process with Customs, set up proper invoicing systems, and trained them on SST compliance. They're now fully compliant and filing on time.

Corporate Tax Optimization

Reducing Tax Burden

The Problem: A profitable company was paying RM 150,000+ in corporate tax annually. They weren't claiming eligible deductions and weren't aware of tax incentives available to their industry.

Our Solution

Conducted a comprehensive tax review and identified RM 50,000 in unclaimed deductions and incentives. Restructured their expenses to maximize tax efficiency and helped them claim Pioneer Status incentives. They now save RM 50K annually on taxes.

Tax Incentive Claims

Tech Company

The Problem: A tech startup investing heavily in R&D wasn't aware they qualified for tax incentives. They were missing out on significant tax savings available to technology companies in Malaysia.

Our Solution

Identified their eligibility for MSC Malaysia and R&D tax incentives. Helped them structure their R&D expenses properly and file incentive claims. They now receive tax exemptions and deductions worth RM 200K+ annually.

LHDN Audit Support

Compliance Issue

The Problem: A business received an LHDN audit notice and panicked. They had incomplete records, couldn't explain certain transactions, and feared penalties and back taxes.

Our Solution

Organized all their records, prepared comprehensive documentation, and represented them during the audit. Explained all transactions clearly and provided supporting evidence. The audit concluded with zero additional tax and no penalties.
