Our Approach

How we solve real problems

Real scenarios from businesses like yours

Fundraising Preparation

Series A Round

The Problem: A tech startup wanted to raise Series A but their financials were incomplete. Investors asked for detailed financial models, unit economics, and growth projections they couldn't produce.

Our Solution

Built comprehensive financial models, calculated unit economics, and created investor-ready financial packages. Prepared them for due diligence with all documentation. They successfully raised RM 5M Series A.

Cash Flow Crisis

Urgent Management

The Problem: A growing business suddenly ran out of cash. They didn't see it coming and couldn't pay suppliers or salaries. They needed immediate cash flow management and a recovery plan.

Our Solution

Analyzed their cash flow, identified the root cause, and created a 90-day recovery plan. Negotiated payment terms with suppliers, optimized collections, and secured bridge funding. They're now cash-flow positive with 6-month runway.

Growth Strategy

Scaling Planning

The Problem: A profitable business wanted to expand but didn't know if they could afford it. They needed to understand the financial impact of hiring, opening new locations, or launching new products.

Our Solution

Built financial models for different growth scenarios. Showed them exactly how much capital they needed, when they'd break even, and what the ROI would be. They expanded confidently and hit profitability targets.

Investor Reporting

Board Presentations

The Problem: A funded startup struggled to produce board-ready financial reports. Their investors wanted monthly financial updates, KPIs, and strategic insights they couldn't deliver.

Our Solution

Set up automated monthly financial reporting with KPIs, variance analysis, and strategic insights. Now they deliver board-ready reports on time, every month. Investors are impressed with their financial discipline.
