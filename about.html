Our story

We started Amjad & Hazli because we saw too many Malaysian founders struggling with outdated accounting practices. Spreadsheets passed around on WhatsApp. Tax deadlines missed. Financial blind spots that cost real money.

As chartered accountants with experience across Big 4 firms, multinational corporations, and venture-backed startups, we knew there had to be a better way.

"We've been on both sides—as the accountants preparing the numbers, and as founders desperately needing them. That dual perspective shapes everything we do."

Today, we serve high-growth businesses across Malaysia—from bootstrapped startups to venture-backed companies raising their Series A. Our AI handles the repetitive work. Our team handles the judgment calls. You get clarity, compliance, and peace of mind.
